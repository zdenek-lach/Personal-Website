So you want to know more..

My name is Zdeněk, I'm 21 years old, I'm a student at Ostravská Univerzita. I live in Frýdek-Místek district in a family house.

My computer has i7-8700k overclocked to 5 GHz. 16 Gb of RGB ram.. RGB is important!
GPU needs an update, it's old-ish MSI GTX 970.

I can program in Java and C#. I can also create websites using HTML, CSS, and some JavaScript. One of my free-time hobbies is modeling in Blender, editing photos in Photoshop and videos in Premiere.

My car is Volkswagen Golf IV 1.8T. Full LED, otherwise stock.

I love my girlfriend. ♥
